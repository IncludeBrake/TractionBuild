# Salem Pricing Guide (Enterprise-Ready)

## Overview
Salem delivers a compliance-first, AI-driven marketing and analytics platform with enterprise-grade reliability, security, and scalability. This pricing guide is designed for elite enterprises, balancing predictability, compliance, and innovative features while ensuring profitability.

## Pricing Structure

### 1. User’s API Costs
- **Description**: Clients cover their own API usage costs (e.g., third-party integrations, data feeds).
- **Exception**: The **Control Panel** chatroom, enabling real-time collaboration with Salem’s AI agents (Strategist, Market Analyst, etc.), is included at no extra cost.
- **Why?**: Aligns with enterprise expectations for cost transparency and avoids subsidizing variable usage.

### 2. Project Build Cost (One-Time)
- **Description**: A fixed upfront fee for design, development, and deployment of Salem’s platform tailored to client needs.
- **Inclusions**:
  - Custom marketing asset setup (campaigns, creatives).
  - Analytics infrastructure (dashboards, attribution models).
  - Backend logging and monitoring (security, uptime, drift detection).
- **Pricing**: Starts at $50,000 (SMB) to $150,000 (enterprise), based on complexity and scale.
- **Why?**: Covers initial CAPEX, ensures bespoke integration, and aligns with enterprise budgets for one-time investments.

### 3. Monthly Maintenance Tiers
- **Description**: Recurring fees for ongoing platform operation, monitoring, and compliance, offered in three tiers to match enterprise needs.
- **Tiers**:
  - **Core Tier** ($5,000/month):
    - Salem’s **constant watch** over:
      - Marketing assets (campaign updates, creative refreshes).
      - Analytics (traffic, conversion, attribution dashboards).
      - Backend logging (uptime, performance monitoring).
    - Basic compliance (GDPR/CCPA data handling, annual audit).
    - Standard support (8/5 email, 48-hour response SLA).
  - **Pro Tier** ($12,000/month):
    - All Core Tier features, plus:
    - Enhanced compliance (SOC2 Type II, quarterly audits, data residency options).
    - Security updates (API key rotation, encryption upgrades, monthly vulnerability scans).
    - Priority support (24/7 email/chat, 12-hour response SLA).
    - Predictive analytics for campaign optimization (e.g., ROI forecasts).
  - **Elite Tier** ($25,000/month):
    - All Pro Tier features, plus:
    - White-glove compliance (dedicated audits, EU AI Act adherence, real-time regulatory updates).
    - Advanced security (zero-knowledge proofs, per August 20, 2025 discussion, for data provenance).
    - Dedicated account manager (4-hour incident response SLA).
    - AI-driven insights (causal attribution, per August 20, 2025, for “WOW” marketing impact).
- **Why?**:
  - Tiered structure caters to SMBs (Core) and enterprises (Pro/Elite), ensuring scalability.
  - Compliance and security are embedded in Pro/Elite Tiers, meeting enterprise “have to haves” without separate fees.
  - Extras (predictive analytics, causal attribution) differentiate Salem in a saturated market.

### 4. Additional Feature Costs
- **Description**: New features (e.g., A/B testing platforms, custom analytics modules) incur a one-time development fee.
- **Pricing**: $10,000–$50,000 per feature, based on complexity.
- **Post-Delivery**: Reverts to the client’s chosen maintenance tier.
- **Why?**: Ensures flexibility for customizations while maintaining predictable OPEX.

### 5. Enterprise Must-Haves & Extras
- **Must-Haves** (Included in Pro/Elite Tiers):
  - **Compliance**: GDPR, CCPA, SOC2 Type II, EU AI Act (per August 20, 2025 discussion), with quarterly audit reports and data deletion protocols.
  - **Security**: API key rotation, encryption (AES-256), immutable audit logs, annual penetration testing.
  - **SLAs**: Defined response times (12-hour for Pro, 4-hour for Elite) and 99.9% uptime guarantee.
  - **Auditability**: Structured logs, immutable audit stores (per August 31, 2025 discussion), and compliance dashboards.
- **Extras** (Pro/Elite Tiers):
  - Predictive analytics for campaign ROI (Pro/Elite).
  - Causal attribution for marketing impact (Elite, per August 20, 2025).
  - White-glove onboarding (Elite): Dedicated setup, training, and 30-day pilot.
  - Real-time regulatory updates (Elite): Proactive monitoring of EU AI Act, US state laws (per August 24, 2025).

### 6. Profitability Guardrails
- **Cost-Plus Pricing**: Minimum 30% margin on all tiers to cover OPEX (compliance tools, legal reviews, support staff).
- **Scalable Infrastructure**: Use cost-efficient tools (e.g., Redis, Neo4j, per August 14, 2025) to minimize hosting costs.
- **Upsell Path**: Core → Pro → Elite progression encourages clients to upgrade for premium features, boosting revenue.
- **Pilot Programs**: Offer 30-day pilots (Elite Tier) to reduce churn and prove ROI, ensuring long-term contracts.

## Why Enterprises Will Love It
- **Predictability**: Tiered pricing aligns with OPEX budgets, with clear deliverables and no hidden fees.
- **Compliance-First**: Embedded GDPR/SOC2/EU AI Act adherence (per August 20, 2025) meets regulatory demands.
- **Differentiation**: Predictive analytics and causal attribution (per August 20, 2025) provide “WOW” value, setting Salem apart.
- **Flexibility**: Core Tier suits SMBs, while Pro/Elite cater to enterprises, ensuring broad appeal.
- **Trust**: Transparent SLAs, audit trails, and white-glove support (Elite) build confidence.

## Notes for Sub-Projects
- Sub-projects created for clients must adopt this pricing structure to ensure consistency, compliance, and scalability.
- Use the same tiered model (Core/Pro/Elite) for sub-project maintenance to streamline operations and maintain enterprise trust.
